Digital Balance Kids Foundation
Protecting children from the dangers of technology use one day at a time. Join our mission to bring a technology free environment back to childhood.
MISSION
For the past few years, more and more news highlights regarding children being negatively impacted by technology becomes more prevalent. While society rapidly continues to utilize technology, children at a younger age have more access than ever. We believe at Digital Balance that technology use has more negative impacts than positive on children. Join us and our mission to disable technology use in young children's homes and schools through our program.
PROGRAM
Once you sign up with Digital Balance, we receive access to all of your children's devices and have the power to disable them. We also have a community petition with local school systems to get rid of technology devices such as iPads, laptops, smartphones, and smartboards across all classrooms. We believe at Digital Balance that technology does not propel the development of our youth.
